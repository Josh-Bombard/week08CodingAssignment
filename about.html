Risk of Rain 2 Objective

Risk of Rain is a roguelike game where you complete stages and have to defeat a final boss and escape the moon! There's multiple characters that you can play as and they all have unique playstyles. There's also lots of unique items that give you anything from shooting missles, using guitairs to shoot electricity, and being able to fly! As you explore each stage there's new enemies to face, chests to loot, and floor bosses to kill! Over time, the games difficulty increases based off of how long you've been doing a run. The game has many different playing styles and never gets boring!
